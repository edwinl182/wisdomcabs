About Us

Wisdom Cabs is premier Car Rental Company headquartered in Bangalore and Service network in major cities across India. We from inception are a Technology driven Company with innovative approach in Car Rental Industry with a state-of-art IT facility for our efficient operations and uninterrupted service to customers.

Formerly known as Newtrack Cabs (NTC) started in 1999 and market presence of 15+ Years, the organisation is lead by a proficient board of Directors with an average Industrial Experience of 20+ Years.



Our Vision

To build a technology driven firm with ease to manage not only impeccable service quality but also to give the best experience to our customers.

Our Mission

We will strive to expand our people logistics and network to maximum capacity and strengthen our financial capability through servicing various industries, business verticals, and individuals therefore, offering both B2B & B2C services.

We provide variety of cabs in and across destinations with a fleet of Premium, Luxury and Popular Car Models at affordable cost. We also offer a round the clock service with a 24/7 Customer Support System.
